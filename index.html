about me

Hello! I'm Vlad Bibire, a passionate UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer from United Kingdom. With over 5 years experience as a Graphic Designer and over 2 years as a Coder, I can confidently say that my goal now is to inspire others to embrace their passion and make the most out of it. For me, everything started when I was 13 years old when I was learning how to create professional flayers like a copy-shop does and then I started to learn how to code and give a meaning to my websites mockups.
